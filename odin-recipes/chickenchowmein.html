Chicken Chowmein

Ingredients: 8 oz (225g) chow mein noodles or thin egg noodles 2 tablespoons vegetable oil 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced 1 cup shredded cabbage 1/2 cup julienned carrots 1/2 cup bean sprouts 3 green onions, sliced 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
Sauce: 3 tablespoons soy sauce 2 tablespoons oyster sauce 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce 1 tablespoon sesame oil 1 tablespoon cornstarch 1/2 cup chicken broth (or water)
Instructions: Prepare the noodles: Cook noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside. Make the sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, sesame oil, cornstarch, and chicken broth until smooth. Cook the chicken: Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the sliced chicken and stir-fry for 4-5 minutes until fully cooked. Remove chicken and set aside. Cook the vegetables: In the same skillet, add another tablespoon of oil. Stir-fry the garlic, ginger, carrots, and cabbage for 2-3 minutes until just tender. Combine everything: Add the cooked noodles, chicken, and sauce to the skillet. Toss everything together and stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until heated through and well coated. Finish: Stir in the bean sprouts and green onions. Cook for 1 more minute. Remove from heat. Serve: Serve hot, optionally garnished with extra green onions or sesame seeds.
